About

What is GirlScript

GirlScript is a project that started under Mozilla Open Leadership Program and mentored by Cambridge University.

Soon, with positive results it got registered as a non profit organisation under Government of India.

Vision & Mission

Spreading awareness related to international events & conference Helping you and other people reach there, getting scholarships and fellowships People in final year of technical course like Engineering are buying their projects, so we want to stop this trend Also, 80% people think that there is no life beyond C, C++ and Java. Plus, they think that to get a job in technical field they need to be a hard core programmers but it is not true and we want to remove those myths. Next is, women are toppers in college but when it comes to jobs, they don't prefer to go for a tech jobs, as they have several fears.

Goal

Want to become biggest platform to learn and help clueless beginners, as there are many opportunities but no skills. Soon we will start helping the children who are tech maniacs and we are working on that project too.
